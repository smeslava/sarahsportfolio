Gardens Are Great!

I love a good garden! I love hydrangeas, lilacs, dogwoods, and magnolias. We have two dogwood trees and two red twig dogwood bushes. We have a magnolia tree, and two lilacs (one is a Miss Kim variety, so it is a dwarf but the other is full size). Hydrangeas might be a bit of an obsession, we have 5! I strive to plant varietals that are unique or interesting. For instance, one of our red twig dogwoods is variegated so the leaves are green and white instead of solid green.
