We Decorate Like Hogwarts at Halloween

At Halloween we turn our front windowsill into a Harry Potter display with the floating candles from the great hall. We fill the window seat with pumpkins and I have cobbled together a Hermione costume (although I do have a wand!) and I am always in awe of other people’s HP inspired displays. The one above is from a toy shop in McMinnville, Oregon where they were displaying their “wands” in a very Ollivander’s style.
